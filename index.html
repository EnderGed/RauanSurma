Hi, I'm Rauan

I’m Rauan, and I believe that the most beautiful moments in life are the simple, everyday ones: like the morning chaos of getting the kids ready for kindergarten or the quiet connection shared during an evening walk. For me, finding these moments of beauty is more than a hobby; it’s a way of life that brings me joy and meaning.

My goal is to capture your family’s story with the same care and appreciation I have for these everyday treasures. I want you to feel completely comfortable and at ease, so you can forget the camera is even there. My promise is a cinematic film that’s truly about you and your loved ones, a beautiful and honest reflection of your life, without any pressure to pose or perform. I’m here to cherish and protect your memories, preserving them for you to relive and share for years to come.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me training my cat Gaston or exploring new parks with my own family.
